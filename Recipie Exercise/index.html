gulaab jammun

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 2 tbsp maida
  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • Milk (as needed)
  • Oil for frying
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • Cardamom

Process

  1. Mix milk powder, maida, ghee, and milk to form dough.
  2. Make small balls and deep fry.
  3. Boil sugar, water, cardamom to make syrup.
  4. Soak fried balls in warm syrup.

Carrot Halwa

Ingredients

  • 2 cups grated carrot
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 4 tbsp sugar
  • Cardamom, dry fruits

Process

  1. Heat ghee, add carrot.
  2. Cook in milk till soft.
  3. Add sugar, cardamom.
  4. Cook till thick, add dry fruits.

Burfi

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • Cardamom powder

Process

  1. Heat ghee in a pan.
  2. Add milk, sugar, and milk powder.
  3. Stir until thick.
  4. Add cardamom, mix well.
  5. Spread on a tray, cool, and cut.
